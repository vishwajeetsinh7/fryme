About Fry Me

Fry Me has been crafting delicious fryums and papads for over a decade. With innovation and quality at our core, we serve businesses that bring joy to millions of snack lovers across India.

Our state-of-the-art manufacturing facility in Howrah ensures consistent quality and timely delivery to our partners nationwide. With over 10 years of experience, we understand the needs of businesses and deliver products that meet the highest standards.

From traditional fryums to innovative pasta and noodle products, our diverse range caters to various business requirements. We work closely with our B2B partners to ensure their success in the market.
